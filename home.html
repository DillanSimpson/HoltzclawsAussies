Holtzclaws Aussies is located 30 minutes north of Columbia, MO. We have been breeding Austrialian Shepards since 2015.
All of our puppies are AKC certified, microchiped and vaccinated. We take care of duclaw removal as well as the tail docking. If you have an questions about our puppies please feel free to visit our facebook page or inquire with us!
